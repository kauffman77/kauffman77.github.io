HW Grade Scoring

For HW grades, enter the total points up to 110 (best 11 / 13 HWs). Most HW quizzes are worth 10 points. Drop the lowest 2 scores and total the remaining HW scores.

Engagement Points

  • Labs are worth 1 Engagement Point per completion. The total possible labs is listed. Partial credit may be assigned for some labs which should be added to the total completed.
  • Bonus Engagement Points can be earned through other means such participation in lecture and the discussion board (awarded via "bonus cards", 3 cards = 1 Engagement point). Canvas will list a "Bonus Engagement Points" column with totals for these.
  • Project Late Days: Engagement points are deducted for each day late a project is submitted. Canvas will list a "Project Late Days" column which should be subtracted from the total Engagement Points.
  • Computed Engagement Points = Labs + Bonus - Late.
    Full credit is earned by obtaining 10 Engagement Points for 10%. Each point beyond this earns 0.25% in Bonus Credit.
