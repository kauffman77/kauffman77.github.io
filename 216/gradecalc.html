Project Makeup Credit

Some projects featured MAKEUP CREDIT which needs to be applied manually. If you lost points on one project but had makeup credit on another, manually redistribute credit. Example: P1 was 93 / 100, P2 was 110 / 100; enter P1: 100, P2: 100.

Engagement Points

Engagement Points are bonus credit earned from:
  • Lecture participation
  • Discussion Review Participation
  • Participation on Piazza: 1 EP per student answer answer, 1 per "good question / answer / comment" staff endorsement
  • On time Project Submission: 2 EPs for ontime, 1 EP for within 1 day late
  • Completing the Midterm and Exit Surveys.
The Weighted Score contributed by EPs is:
EP Weighted Score = 0.50% * floor(log2(TotalEPs + 1))
